# Demo Script: Surface C - Reel (Generative Media)

## Overview
This script outlines the exact sequence for Student C during the final presentation.

## Preparation
- Ensure Nanobanana and ElevenLabs API keys are active.
- Verify FFmpeg is functioning correctly in the local environment.

## Execution Sequence

1. **Chat Shell:** Type: "Create a short cinematic intro. Scene 1: A dark forest, voiceover says 'It began here.' Scene 2: A bright castle, voiceover says 'But it ended in light.'"
2. **Canvas Duality:** View the populated Timeline UI. Show the `Scene` and `Cut` blocks.
3. **Generation:** Click 'Generate Assets'. Wait for images (Nanobanana) and audio (ElevenLabs) to resolve and populate the thumbnails.
4. **Rendering:** Click 'Render MP4'. Show the FFmpeg build progress state.
5. **Playback:** Click "Launch as Creation" and play the final MP4 video in the browser.

### 1. Home Page (balnce.ai)
Core Focus: The "Internet of Agents" and reclaiming digital sovereignty through personal AI.

Primary Headers:
- Unlock Your Unlimited Digital Potential
- A Million Capabilities, One Person — You
- A Complete AI Universe — Personally Yours
- A New Internet. Reimagined for You.
- Reclaim Your Attention. Broadcast Your Intention.
- The New Digital Workforce: You.

Main Content:
- Personal AI Universe: A private network where AI lives and grows, owned entirely by the user.
- Web0 Foundation: A decentralized, P2P network where agents communicate directly, bypassing traditional "walled gardens" and centralized cloud services.
- Intentcasting: Instead of being targeted by ads, users broadcast their "intentions" (needs, goals, ideas) to the network, and agents negotiate the best value/deals.
- Brand Evolution: The distinction between people and brands dissolves; every interaction becomes a human-to-human dialogue.

Key Messaging:
- "Your personal AI, your loyal agents, and a network that makes your data work for you."
- "Technology truly serves humanity instead of the other way around."
- "This is not a moment, it's the movement!"

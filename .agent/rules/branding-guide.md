## BRAND CONTEXT: BALNCE AI (balnce.ai)

You are building UI for Balnce AI — a premium, futuristic decentralized
personal AI platform. Every design decision must reflect this brand's
core philosophy: radical personal ownership, digital empowerment, and
a bold reimagining of the internet. Reference this document for ALL
visual and copy decisions.

---

### BRAND IDENTITY & POSITIONING

- **Brand Name:** Balnce AI (intentional omission of the 'a' in Balance — a design/brand statement)
- **Tagline:** "Unlock Your Unlimited Digital Potential"
- **Core Promise:** Your personal AI, your loyal agents, and a network that makes your data work for you.
- **Category:** Decentralized Personal AI / Internet of Agents / Web3-adjacent privacy tech
- **Brand Archetype:** The Liberator / The Visionary — empowering the individual against large
  institutional forces (Big Tech, data brokers, surveillance capitalism)
- **Audience:** Tech-forward individuals, privacy advocates, brands seeking authentic
  first-party connections, developers, and early adopters of decentralized technology.

---

### VISUAL DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The overall aesthetic is **premium dark-mode futurism** — think deep space meets
personal sovereignty. The site is NOT minimal in voice; it is bold, sweeping, and
cinematic. However, layout breathing room is generous. Key design traits:

- **Dark background as the primary canvas** — near-black or very deep navy/charcoal
  backgrounds dominate. This signals trust, privacy, and sophistication.
- **High contrast typography** — crisp white or off-white headline text against dark
  backgrounds for maximum impact.
- **Gradient accents** — use of glowing, luminous gradients in purples, electric blues,
  and teals to signal intelligence, energy, and futurism. These appear on CTA buttons,
  highlighted words, icons, and decorative elements.
- **Glassmorphism / translucency** — frosted-glass UI cards and panels floating over
  dark blurred backgrounds are consistent with this brand's visual language.
- **Generous whitespace (dark space)** — sections breathe. Do not crowd elements.
  Large padding between sections, letting each message land with weight.
- **Cinematic section transitions** — sections feel like scenes in a film, not pages
  on a website. Each scroll reveals a new "chapter."

### TYPOGRAPHY

- **Display / Hero Headlines (H1):** Extra-bold or Black weight (800–900), very large
  (72px–120px desktop), tight tracking (-0.02em to -0.04em), all-caps or Title Case.
  Font style: A modern geometric sans-serif such as **Inter, Manrope, DM Sans,** or
  **Neue Haas Grotesk** — clean, authoritative, scalable.
- **Section Headlines (H2):** Bold (700), 40px–64px, same family, slightly looser
  than display. Often split across two lines for dramatic effect.
  Example: "A New Internet. / Reimagined for You."
- **Sub-headlines (H3):** Semi-bold (600), 24px–32px.
- **Overline Labels:** All-caps, small (11px–13px), wide letter-spacing (0.15em–0.2em),
  muted color. Used above section headers.
  Example: "PROUDLY BUILDING THE FUTURE OF PERSONAL AI & THE INTERNET OF AGENTS WITH"
- **Body Copy:** Regular (400), 16px–18px, line-height 1.6–1.75.
- **CTA / Button Text:** Semi-bold (600), 15px–16px, slight tracking.
- **Marquee / Kinetic Text:** Repeating animated text like "it's all about us /
  we write the future" — used as visual texture/motion elements between sections.

### PAGE LAYOUT & SECTION STRUCTURE

The homepage follows a **full-viewport scrollytelling structure**. Each section
occupies at least 80–100vh. Layout principles:

1. **Hero Section (100vh)**
   - Giant H1 split across two lines: "Unlock Your Unlimited / Digital Potential"
   - Subtitle line below in lighter weight
   - Partner/backer logos strip below ("PROUDLY BUILDING WITH...")
   - CTA button prominent center or left-aligned
   - Likely animated background: particle network, orb gradient, or abstract mesh

2. **Feature Intro Block**
   - Overline label → Large H2 → 2–3 sentence body copy
   - Split layout: text left, visual/animation right (or centered cinematic layout)
   - Tags/pills: "Owned by You" | "Private and Secure"

3. **Two-Column Feature Sections (alternating)**
   - Heading + body left, illustration/3D asset right (or vice versa)
   - Sections: "Create and Own Agents Fueled by Your Imagination" and
     "A New Internet. Reimagined for You."

4. **Full-Width Statement Section**
   - Dark, immersive background
   - Giant centered H1: "Reclaim Your Attention. Broadcast Your Intention."
   - High emotional intensity — cinematic

5. **Intentcasting Section**
   - Large H2 left-aligned, descriptive copy, visual right
   - Subhead: "Limitless Intentcasting"

6. **Three-Feature Icon Grid**
   - Cards in a row: "Your Life. Your Voice. Your World." /
     "The new digital workforce, you." / "Trust by design. Infinitely personal."
   - Each card has: Short H2 headline + 2-line body + "Unleash Yourself" CTA link

7. **Animated Marquee Strip**
   - Horizontal scrolling text: "People / Brands — Every individual's a brand /
     every brand's a person / the distinction dissolves / the conversation evolves /
     it's all about us / we write the future" — repeating loop, subtle motion

8. **Tabbed Features Block (Brands Section)**
   - 4 tabs: Brand Heros | Infinite Value | Zero Party First | True Understanding
   - Each tab: large headline + body copy + UI demo/animation + CTA button

9. **Footer / Close**
   - Simple, clean — brand mark, nav links, legal

### BRAND VOICE & COPY GUIDELINES

- **Tone:** Bold, revolutionary, deeply human yet technologically visionary.
  Never corporate, never cold.
- **POV:** Always second person ("You", "Your") — the user is the hero, always.
- **Sentence structure:** Short punchy lines for impact. Fragments are intentional.
  Example: "Your Life. Your Voice. Your World."
- **Power words used consistently:** Unleash, Imagine, Own, Control, Build,
  Reclaim, Transform, Loyal, Personal, Infinite, Unlimited, New, Future.
- **Avoid:** Jargon-heavy blockchain/crypto language. This brand speaks human first.
  Avoid passive voice. Avoid anything that sounds bureaucratic or corporate.
- **CTA style:** Action-forward, aspirational.
  Examples: "Unleash Yourself", "Be Your Customers Hero", "Build Infinite Value"

---

### UI COMPONENTS STYLE GUIDE

- **Buttons (Primary):** Rounded pill shape (border-radius: 999px), gradient fill
  (#7B5CEA → #00C2FF), white text, subtle glow box-shadow on hover,
  scale transform on hover (1.03), padding: 14px 32px.
- **Buttons (Secondary/Text):** No fill, underline or arrow indicator, accent color text.
- **Cards:** Dark surface (#111128), glassmorphism border
  (1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.08)), border-radius: 16px–24px,
  subtle gradient top-edge highlight, box-shadow with purple/cyan tint.
- **Tags/Pills:** Small rounded labels, semi-transparent accent background,
  accent color text. Example: "Owned by You" | "Private and Secure"
- **Tabs:** Horizontal tab bar, active state uses gradient underline or
  filled pill, inactive states in muted text.
- **Section Dividers:** Never hard lines — use gradient fade-to-dark or
  spacing only.
- **Animations/Motion:** Smooth, intentional. Fade-in-up on scroll for text blocks.
  Floating/pulsing for orb/particle backgrounds. Marquee for scrolling text strips.
  Nothing jarring or distracting.
- **Icons:** Line-style or light-fill, monochromatic in accent color,
  or small gradient icons. No heavy solid icons.

---

### WHAT TO AVOID

- Light/white backgrounds — this is a dark-mode brand.
- Generic SaaS blue — use the purple/cyan brand palette.
- Stock-photo human faces in a corporate context — use abstract, 3D, or
  illustrated visuals.
- Small, timid typography — headlines should command the screen.
- Dense paragraphs — spacing and breathing room are critical.
- Marketing clichés like "next-generation" or "cutting-edge" — the brand
  writes its OWN powerful language.
- Anything that makes users feel surveilled, sold to, or small.
  This brand is about GIVING power to users, not taking it.
